Affirmative Constructive (6 minutes): The affirmative side presents their case. Cross-Examination by Negative (3 minutes): The negative side questions the affirmative. Negative Constructive (7 minutes): The negative side presents their case and addresses the affirmative's arguments. Cross-Examination by Affirmative (3 minutes): The affirmative side questions the negative. First Affirmative Rebuttal (4 minutes): The affirmative side responds to the negative's arguments. Negative Rebuttal (6 minutes): The negative side responds to the affirmative's rebuttal. Second Affirmative Rebuttal (3 minutes): The affirmative side gives their final response.
