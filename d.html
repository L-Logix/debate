Logical Reasoning

Logical reasoning is very important when you are trying to ensure that the audience understands your message. If your message is not logical, people may consider you untrustworthy. Being logical can sometimes stall your opponent, as they try to unravel the bizarre sense that your argument makes. Good Example: All men are mortals Ben is a man Therefore, Ben is a mortal. Since Ben is a man, he is also a mortal. Bad Example: All men are mortals Erin is a mortal Therefore, Erin is a man. Just because Erin is mortal, she does not have to be a man.
